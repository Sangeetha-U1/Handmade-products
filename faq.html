Orders & Payment

Placing an order is easy! Browse our shop, select the product you love, choose the quantity, and click "Add to Cart". Once you're ready, go to your cart and click "Proceed to Checkout". You can pay using UPI, credit/debit card, net banking, or Cash on Delivery. You'll receive an order confirmation email immediately after placing your order.

We accept a wide range of payment methods for your convenience: all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), net banking, EMI options for orders above ₹3,000, and Cash on Delivery for orders up to ₹10,000 within India.

You can modify or cancel your order within 24 hours of placing it by contacting our support team at support@mittiandmann.com or calling +91 98765 43210. After 24 hours, orders enter the packaging stage and may not be cancellable. Customized or made-to-order items cannot be cancelled once production begins.

On the Cart page, you'll find a "Coupon Code" field. Enter your code and click "Apply". The discount will be automatically deducted from your total. Please note that only one coupon can be applied per order and codes cannot be combined with other ongoing promotions unless specifically stated.
Shipping & Delivery

Standard delivery takes 3–7 business days for most locations within India. Metro cities (Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad) typically receive orders in 3–4 days. Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities may take up to 7 days. Remote areas or union territories may take 8–12 business days. You'll receive a tracking link via email and SMS once your order ships.

Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all orders above ₹999 within India. For orders below ₹999, a flat shipping fee of ₹99 applies. We also offer express delivery (1–2 days) for an additional charge of ₹199, available in select cities.

Yes, we ship to 30+ countries including USA, UK, Canada, Australia, UAE, and Singapore. International shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on your location and order weight. International orders typically take 10–18 business days. Please note that customs duties and taxes are the buyer's responsibility.

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email and SMS with a tracking number and a link to track your package in real-time. You can also track your order by logging into your account and visiting "My Orders". If you don't receive a tracking update within 3 business days of your order, please contact us.
Products & Quality

Absolutely! Every single product on Mitti & Mann is 100% handmade by real artisans. We personally visit artisan clusters across India to source our products. Each item comes with a certificate of authenticity and a handwritten card from the artisan who made it. Minor variations in color, texture, and size are natural signs of genuine handcraft — they're features, not flaws!

Care instructions vary by product type. Pottery and ceramics should be hand-washed and avoided from microwave unless specified. Textiles should be gently hand-washed or dry-cleaned. Wooden items should be kept away from direct sunlight and moisture; polish occasionally with natural oil. Jewelry should be stored in a dry place and cleaned with a soft cloth. Specific care cards are included with each purchase.

Returns & Refunds

We offer a 7-day return policy for most products. To be eligible, items must be unused, in their original packaging, and in the same condition you received them. To initiate a return, email support@mittiandmann.com with your order number and photos of the product. Once approved, we'll arrange a free pickup. Customized, on-sale, and fragile items marked as "final sale" are not eligible for returns.

We take great care in packaging all fragile items. However, if your product arrives damaged, please email us within 48 hours of delivery at support@mittiandmann.com with clear photos of the damage and the packaging. We'll arrange a replacement or full refund at no extra cost to you. Please do not discard the original packaging — we may ask you to return it.

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll notify you within 2 business days. If approved, refunds are processed within 5–7 business days. The amount will be credited to your original payment method. For UPI and bank transfers, it may take an additional 2–3 business days depending on your bank. Store credits are instant.
About Our Artisans

Our artisan relations team personally travels to craft clusters and villages across India to discover and vet artisans. We look for authentic traditional skills, sustainable practices, and genuine craftsmanship passed down through generations. We also verify fair labor practices and ensure safe working conditions. Every artisan partner signs our Fair Trade agreement before their products are listed on our platform.

When you purchase from Mitti & Mann, 65–75% of the product price goes directly to the artisan — significantly higher than the industry average of 20–30%. We also provide artisans with design support, packaging, and access to a global market they couldn't reach on their own. Additionally, we fund skill development workshops and connect younger generations with master craftspeople.
