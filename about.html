How We Started

Born in the Lanes of Jaipur

In 2018, our founder Kavita Rathore was walking through the narrow lanes of Jaipur when she encountered a potter named Ram Prasad quietly shaping clay on a wheel in the corner of a crumbling haveli. His work was extraordinary — each pot bore the fingerprints of generations of knowledge — yet he could barely earn enough to feed his family.

That encounter changed everything. Kavita left her corporate job in Mumbai and founded Mitti & Mann (meaning "Earth & Soul" in Hindi) — a platform dedicated to bringing authentic handmade crafts from rural Indian artisans to people who truly value them.

Today, we work with over 200 artisan families across 14 states, ensuring fair wages, dignified work conditions, and a global audience for their extraordinary craftsmanship.

Our Mission

To create a sustainable livelihood for India's skilled craftspeople by connecting them with a global audience that appreciates authentic, handmade goods — while preserving our rich cultural craft heritage for future generations.

🌱

Our Vision

A world where "handmade" is not just a label but a valued choice — where every artisan earns dignity and recognition for their craft, and every home holds at least one object that carries the spirit of human creativity.
